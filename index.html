FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you start learning with Cognito.

What is Cognito?

Cognito is an AI-powered learning app for iPhone and iPad. It turns YouTube videos, PDFs, lectures, photos, web pages, and arXiv papers into flashcards and structured Skill Tree learning programs, then schedules reviews with the FSRS spaced-repetition algorithm so you remember more in less time.

Is Cognito free?

Yes. Manual card creation, FSRS spaced repetition, Classic and Compact study modes, Anki/CSV/TSV import, and up to 10 AI generations per day are free forever. Premium unlocks unlimited AI generation, Skill Trees, and advanced content sources.

What can Cognito turn into flashcards?

Type any topic, or upload a YouTube video, PDF, audio lecture, photo of your notes, web page, or arXiv paper. The AI extracts the key concepts and creates study-ready cards automatically.

Is Cognito a good Anki alternative?

Yes. Cognito uses the same modern FSRS scheduling algorithm and imports your existing Anki (.apkg), CSV, and TSV decks, while adding AI card generation, Skill Trees, and a native iPhone and iPad design.

How much does Premium cost?

Premium is $9.99 per month or $39.99 per year, billed through the App Store. A free trial is available, and you can cancel anytime in your Apple ID settings.

Which devices does Cognito support?

Cognito runs on iPhone and iPad with iOS 18 or later. Your cards, progress, and settings sync across your devices through iCloud.

Is my data private?

Your flashcards and study history are stored on your device and your private iCloud. AI features send only the content you actively submit to Google's Gemini API, and Cognito uses no ads or third-party tracking. See our Privacy Policy for details.
