Questions, answered

Is Cognito really free?+

Yes. Manual cards, FSRS spaced repetition, Classic & Compact study, Anki/CSV/TSV import, iCloud sync, and 3 AI generations a day are free forever — no account needed. Premium adds unlimited AI and the advanced sources and tools.

What is FSRS, and why does it matter?+

FSRS is a modern spaced-repetition scheduler — the same algorithm serious Anki users switch to. It models your personal forgetting curve and reviews each card at the optimal moment, so you remember more while studying less.

Can I bring my Anki decks?+

Absolutely. Import your existing Anki (.apkg), CSV, or TSV decks in seconds — cards, decks, and all.

Which devices does it run on?+

iPhone and iPad, on iOS 18 or later, with iCloud sync to keep everything in step across your devices.

Do I need to be online?+

Studying and reviewing work offline. Generating cards with AI needs a connection, since that step runs in the cloud.
