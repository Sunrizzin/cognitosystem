Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Cognito ("the App") is built with privacy in mind. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Local Data Storage

Your flashcards, study history, settings, and progress are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

If you enable iCloud, your data may sync across your devices through Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Anonymous Analytics

Cognito collects anonymous usage events through Firebase Analytics to help us understand how the app is used and improve the experience. These events include:

  • Study sessions (start, completion, accuracy)
  • Feature usage (card creation, AI generation, import)
  • Settings changes (theme, study mode, daily goal)
  • Subscription events (paywall views, purchases)

No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected. Events do not contain your name, email, card content, or any data that could identify you. We use this data solely to improve the App.

AI Features & Google Gemini

AI-powered features (card generation, AI Tutor, Voice Tutor, Quiz Mode, answer checking, diagram analysis) are processed through Google's Gemini API. When you use these features, the following data is sent to Google:

  • Text prompts (topics you want to study)
  • Images and photos you upload for card generation or diagram analysis
  • Documents (PDFs) and audio you provide — uploaded files or in-app recordings — to generate cards
  • Links to YouTube videos and web pages you ask the App to process
  • Microphone audio during a Voice Tutor session, streamed live for real-time conversation
  • Card content for restructuring, rephrasing, or answer validation

This content is sent only when you actively use an AI feature. No user IDs, device identifiers, or personal information are included in these requests. Data is processed according to Google's Gemini API Terms.

Purchases & Subscriptions

Premium features are available via optional subscriptions managed through Apple's StoreKit. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

User Accounts & Authentication

Cognito offers optional user accounts via Sign in with Apple. When you create an account, we store the following data in Google Firebase/Firestore:

  • Your chosen nickname and avatar emoji (public)
  • Your Firebase user ID (anonymous identifier)
  • Account creation and update timestamps
  • Avatar background color preference

If you use Sign in with Apple with email relay, we receive only a relay email address managed by Apple. We do not use or share this email for marketing or any other purpose.

Shared Decks

When you publish a deck to the community, the following data is stored in Firestore and visible to all users:

  • Deck title, description, and tags
  • Card content (front and back text, card type)
  • Your public nickname
  • Download count, creation and update timestamps

You can delete your shared decks at any time. When you delete your account, all associated shared decks are also removed.

Third-Party Services

Cognito integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics (Google)
  • Firebase Authentication — user account management via Sign in with Apple (Google)
  • Firebase Firestore — user profiles and shared deck storage (Google)
  • Firebase AppCheck — app integrity verification (Google)
  • Firebase Crashlytics — anonymous crash reporting and diagnostics (Google)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications (Google)
  • Firebase Remote Config — feature configuration and rollout (Google)
  • Google Gemini API — AI card generation and study features
  • Apple StoreKit — subscription and purchase management

No advertising SDKs, ad networks, or user tracking services are used.

Data We Do Not Collect

Cognito does not collect or store:

  • Real names or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers linked to personal data
  • Browsing or search history
  • Your local flashcard content (stored only on your device, unless you explicitly share it)

Your Rights

If you have a Cognito account, you can:

  • View and edit your profile data (nickname, avatar) at any time
  • Delete any shared decks you've published
  • Delete your account and all associated data from the App settings

For users without an account, your study data is fully under your control on your device. You can delete all app data by removing the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Cognito is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data through the App, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

For questions or concerns about privacy, contact us at info@cognito.homes.
